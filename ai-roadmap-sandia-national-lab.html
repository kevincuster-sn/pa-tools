Executive Summary

Sandia National Lab is positioned to reduce service desk operating spend by activating Now Assist across ITSM, ITOM, CMDB, and Creator. Adoption has not yet started despite contract entitlement, and a CIO-level directive targets measurable AI-driven cost reduction by year-end.

  • Now Assist for Creator Activation: Enable developer productivity in sub-production immediately to accelerate platform delivery and AI agent build-out
  • Phase 1 — Deflection Foundation: Customer-facing deflection (Virtual Agent, AI Search, Multi Turn Catalog, Self Service Deflection) plus KB Generation and agent productivity quick wins
  • Phase 2 — Foundation Hardening: CMDB data quality, ITOM autonomous alert triage, and platform AI agents to harden the foundation for agentic L1
  • Phase 3 — Agentic Automation: The ITSM Agent chain (Triage → Investigate → Wrap-up) and autonomous M365 request fulfillment shift L1 to agent-led resolution

Cumulatively, this roadmap takes Sandia from zero AI consumption to a service desk where the majority of L1 work is deflected at the portal, resolved by AI-assisted agents, or completed autonomously — directly addressing the CIO's operational cost reduction objective.
